Booking Terms & Conditions

Bookings

Once we have recived the inital from we will be in contact with you regarding the details of the process. A deposit will be part of these discussions.

Tour Payments

On receipt of deposit, we will send a booking confirmation letter. The balance of payment is due approx. 90 days prior to tour departure. On occasion it may be necessary for us to request balance of payment earlier due to requirements from tour service providers.

Change of cost and itinerary

We will make every effort to avoid changes to the tour costs and itineraries. Each tour cost is based on exchange rates, fares and tariffs applicable at the time of publication. The actual tour costs will depend on exchange rates and tariffs applicable at the time the bookings are paid for by us. Therefore costs may be subject to change. All tours need a minimum number of guests to operate at the prices listed in the itinerary. If the minimum number is not reached, a tour may still operate with a surcharge. From time to time we may be required to modify the itinerary due to changes to accommodation, local tours, transport, walking conditions or weather. If such modifications are required, this is done so with your needs in mind and to provide the best tour experience. We will provide you with as much notice as possible for itinerary changes and in some instances, such modifications may be subject to an additional cost.

Cancellations

Should you decide to cancel your tour, you must notify Kiboko immediately in writing. Cancellation takes place as soon as we receive your written notification. Please note: if your tour invoice has been issued and you cancel before payment is made, you are still bound by our terms and conditions. The following cancellation fees and time lines will apply:

We accept cancellations up to 30 days prior to the departure. If they cancel after that day, we charge 50% of their trip or 100% of their package in case of Specials.

From time to time, we may be required or it may be necessary to cancel the tour and we will provide you with as much notice as is reasonably possible in such circumstances. If we cancel due to not reaching minimum numbers, then your deposit will be immediately refunded to you in full or you may elect to transfer the deposit to another tour. If we cancel a tour due to tour safety, for example in the event of a natural disaster, we will refund as much of your tour payment as we are able to recoup from the operators. Any non-refundable costs you incur may be subject to Travel Insurance claims.

Travel Insurance

Travel insurance is compulsory and is included in the price of the trips unless stated otherwise.

Age, Health and Fitness

You may require vaccinations for certain trips. If this is the case it will be communicated to you in advance and it is your responsibility to organise and pay for these.

We cannot accept bookings from unaccompanied minors or those who cannot travel independently. While we do not have an upper age limit or fitness level, our walks are often in remote locations and you must be certain you are capable of fully participating in the tour activities as noted in the itinerary. Travellers should be in good health and have an appropriate level of fitness. In preparation for your tour, you should be undertaking walks similar to those described in the itinerary. A Medical Questionnaire must be completed prior to the tour departure. Disclosure of your known medical history and conditions is vital, as these may seriously impact the tour. If you have a medical condition, you will be asked to seek your doctor's consent to participate on the tour. Kiboko reserves the right in its absolute discretion to refuse a traveller the right to participate in a tour on medical or fitness grounds. All medical details will be treated as confidential.

Not included in the tour cost

Unless otherwise specified the following are not included in your tour cost: airfares and associated taxes, heavy or excess baggage, travel insurance, meals not listed in the itinerary, single room surcharge, optional tours and excursions, tips, passport and visa expenses, vaccinations, medical evacuations or items of a personal nature such as drinks, laundry, phone calls, souvenirs, postage.

If you encounter any difficulties during the tour that you feel are our responsibility then you should raise them with the tour leader. If you feel that the issue was not adequately dealt with then you should refer the matter to us, in writing, within 30 days of the tour ending.
